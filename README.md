## ğŸ“– About the Project
Understanding the financial requirements of moving to a new city is crucial. The **Cost of Living** project provides a user-friendly interface to visualize expenses such as:
* Housing & Rent
* Groceries & Markets
* Transportation
* Utilities & Internet
* Leisure & Dining out

This tool aggregates data to give users a clear picture of how much they need to earn to maintain their standard of living in a new location.
